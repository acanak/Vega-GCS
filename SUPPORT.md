# Support Vega GCS

Vega GCS is **open source and free** (AGPL-3.0). The full app and its source are, and always
will be, available to everyone — support tiers never paywall features. They fund continued
development and give backers recognition, priority support, and a say in the roadmap.

## Where to support

- **Patreon:** https://www.patreon.com/cw/aCanak

## Monthly tiers

| Tier | Price | Perks |
|---|---|---|
| **Ground Crew** | $3 | Name in BACKERS, Discord "Supporter" badge, dev log access |
| **Pilot** | $8 | + Roadmap voting, early access to release builds |
| **Captain** | $20 | + Priority support, name in the app's About credits, private support channel |
| **Flight Director** | $50 | + Name/small logo in README & About, monthly Q&A / office hours, roadmap influence |
| **Mission Partner** | $150+ | + Featured logo (README, site, About), monthly 1:1 call, prioritized feature requests, invoice |

Annual billing is offered at a ~10–15% discount on every tier.

## Notes

- **AGPL-3.0:** perks are recognition, support, and services — not exclusive/closed features.
- **Goals/milestones** (e.g. "signed Windows/Linux CI builds", "new vehicle support") are published
  on Patreon so backers can see what their support unlocks for the whole community.
